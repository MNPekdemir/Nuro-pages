Privacy Policy

Nuro • Last updated: 3 July 2026

Nuro is built to respect your privacy. It is a local-first, offline, ad-free app.

What we store

Your dhikr list, counter sessions, statistics, settings, Esma plans, and dua journal notes are stored only on your device. We do not collect personal data, and there is no account to create. Notes you mark as "private" can be protected with a local PIN that never leaves the device.

No ads, no tracking

The app contains no advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics. Your dhikr, plan, and dua activity never leaves your device.

Location (prayer times & qibla)

If you use the prayer-times or qibla features, the app reads your device location only to compute prayer times and the qibla direction on your device. Your location is used on-device and is never sent to us or to any third party. You may decline the permission and set your city manually instead.

Qur'an content

Qur'an text and recitation audio are fetched from public Islamic content providers (alquran.cloud and islamic.network) when you read or listen online, and can be saved for offline use. These are ordinary content requests — no personal data, account, or dhikr activity is ever sent. Bundled sûres work fully offline.

Purchases

Optional "supporter" purchases are handled entirely by Google Play (or the App Store). We never see your payment details, and purchases do not unlock or gate any feature.

Notifications

Optional daily reminders are scheduled locally on your device and require no server connection.

Your control

You can export or permanently delete all of your data at any time from Settings → Data. Uninstalling the app also removes all stored data.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
